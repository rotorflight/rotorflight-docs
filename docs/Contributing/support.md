# Support Us!

We hope you enjoy using Rotorflight. If so please consider supporting us with a [donation to our project](https://opencollective.com/rotorflight/donate). You can do a one time donation or a recurring one.

![Donate to Rotorflight](/img/opencollective-rotorflight.png)

We're using [Open Collective](https://opencollective.com/rotorflight) for managing the financial side of things. They do the book keeping, we do the programming. We like Open Collective, because they offer complete transparency - just like our software.
